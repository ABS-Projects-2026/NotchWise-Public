# Changelog

All notable changes to NotchWise will be documented in this file.

## v1.0.0 — March 2026

Initial release.

### Review

- Hover-to-expand notch with flashcard display
- Flip cards with Space, rate recall with 1–4
- Skip cards (S) and snooze for 1 hour (Z)
- Simple Mode for Bluetooth presentation remotes
- Auto-collapse after rating with configurable delay
- Screen-share invisibility (Zoom, Teams, FaceTime, etc.)
- Focus mode awareness — respects Do Not Disturb
- Configurable daily review and new card limits
- Idle detection — nudges only when you're not in deep focus

### Scheduling

- SM-2 algorithm (classic SuperMemo, Anki-compatible)
- FSRS algorithm (Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler)
- Per-deck algorithm selection
- Configurable learning steps, graduating interval, and ease factors
- Lapse handling with customisable relearning steps

### Import & Card Management

- Import Anki `.apkg` decks with full field mapping
- Quick-create cards from anywhere (global shortcut)
- Drag-and-drop text from any app to create cards
- Support for basic, reversed, and cloze deletion card types
- Rich text and image support on card fronts and backs
- Deck organisation with nested sub-decks
- Tag-based filtering and search

### Sync

- AnkiConnect two-way sync over localhost
- iCloud sync across multiple Macs
- Conflict resolution with last-write-wins and manual merge options

### AI Features

- On-device AI card suggestions from dragged text
- Automatic front/back pair generation
- Cloze deletion suggestions
- Context-aware deck routing based on frontmost app

### Workflow Integration

- Menu bar app — no Dock icon
- Global keyboard shortcuts (customisable)
- Pomodoro timer with cards during break intervals
- Calendar-aware scheduling — fewer nudges during meetings
- Virtual notch for non-notch Macs (iMac, Mac Mini, older MacBooks)
- Multi-display support with per-display notch preference

### Statistics

- Retention rate tracking per deck and overall
- Review streak counter with daily goals
- Due card forecast (7-day and 30-day views)
- Calendar heatmap of review activity
- Time-per-card averages and trends
- Export review data as CSV
