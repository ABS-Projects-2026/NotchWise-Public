## Features

- **Anki Compatible** — Import `.apkg` decks and sync with AnkiConnect over localhost
- **FSRS + SM-2** — Choose between the classic SuperMemo algorithm or the modern Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler
- **Context-Aware Decks** — Auto-switches deck based on your frontmost app (Xcode open? Swift cards)
- **Keyboard & Remote** — Space to flip, 1–4 to rate, S to skip, Z to snooze. Bluetooth remotes fully supported
- **Screen-Share Invisibility** — Auto-hides during Zoom, Teams, and other screen sharing sessions
- **Pomodoro Integration** — Cards surface during break intervals, not during deep focus
- **Statistics Dashboard** — Retention rates, streaks, due forecasts, and calendar heatmap
- **Works on All Macs** — Notch MacBooks use the hardware notch; iMacs, Mac Minis, and older MacBooks get a floating widget
- **AI Card Generation** — Drag text from any app into the notch for on-device card suggestions
- **Privacy-First** — All data stored locally. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry

## How It Works

1. **Hover** — Move your cursor to the notch. A due card appears.
2. **Flip** — Press Space to reveal the answer.
3. **Rate** — Press 1–4 to rate your recall. The card is scheduled.

Three states. Eight seconds. Back to work.
